Then, what I want to be?

Well, I was a kid who had a lot of dreams. For example, I fell into the fashion industry and wanted to be a fashion designer as well, so I learned how to use the sawing machine to make my own clothes. However, when I turned in 18, I gave up to be a Fashion designer, because I wasn't sure that I can live well as a fashion designer. I got passionate, but it wasn't enough to follow my dreams through numerous difficulties.

After that situation, I changed my dreams just like the previous one and it was really hard to decide single job for me, so I reach the compromise to be a web developer. at least, I can do the designer's and developer's works at the same time.

On the other hand, I'm still interesting of fashion industries and if I have time after I became a web developer, then I would like to run my own business of fashion brand. Well, Virgil Abloh who is a fashion director of Louis Vuitton and runs his own business named Off-white was architecture as well. So... I think there is no limit to having various jobs.
